Chili Sauce

Description

Spend 5 minutes making this sauce to enjoy with everything and/or everyone

Ingredients
  • 1 cup tomato sauce
  • ¼ cup brown sugar
  • 2 tablespoons vinegar
  • ¼ teaspoon allspice
Instructions
  1. Stir the tomato sauce, brown sugar, vinegar, and allspice together in a mixing bowl until thoroughly blended.
  2. Pour into a covered container, and refrigerate until ready to use.
